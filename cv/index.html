Professional Summary

PhD Candidate in Biostatistics at UTHealth School of Public Health and Graduate Research Assistant at the Van Loo Lab, MD Anderson Cancer Center. My research focuses on cancer genomics, computational biology, and clinical translation — developing statistical and computational methods to understand tumor evolution, intratumoral heterogeneity, and clonal dynamics across multiple cancer types.
